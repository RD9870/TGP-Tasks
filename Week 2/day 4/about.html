Diana Corin

about me

I'm Diana Corin, founder and lead photographer at Lumen Frame Studio. My work centers on capturing genuine emotion and timeless beauty through natural light and thoughtful composition. Photography has always been my way of telling stories—of people, milestones, and the quiet in-between moments that often go unseen. From the gentle anticipation of maternity sessions to the joy of weddings and the pride of graduations, I believe every chapter deserves to be framed with care. Whether in-studio or on location, I aim to create images that feel authentic, warm, and enduring.
